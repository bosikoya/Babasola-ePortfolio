About Me

About Me

Prior to my postgraduate studies, I honed my skills at the largest distribution company in West Africa. There, I witnessed firsthand the transformative potential of data analysis. My experience culminated in a pivotal project where we successfully automated a process, leading to a remarkable 20% increase in revenue - a testament to the powerful synergy between IT and data.

This experience ignited a passion within me to leverage data analytics for positive societal impact. I am particularly interested in exploring how data can be used to improve business margins while simultaneously enhancing the quality of life for the customers they serve.

My Story

My journey began in 2015, ignited by the dynamic world of IT. However, a deeper passion for data soon emerged. Numbers held a captivating allure, each discovery a thrilling step in unraveling hidden patterns. Yet, a sense of incompleteness remained.

The missing piece clicked into place with the realization of a love for human connection. Connecting teams and helping businesses brought together the worlds of data, technology, and people. This confluence sparked my Ikigai, the Japanese concept of reason for being. It's the sweet spot where passion, mission, and profession converge. Now, I leverage my technical skills and data expertise to empower businesses, fulfilling my purpose and fueling my passion.
